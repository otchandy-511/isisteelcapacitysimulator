ISI Steel Capacity Simulator — User Guide
Version 6 · Built for ISI Steel pipe production planning
How the model works
The simulator calculates monthly pipe production capacity for all 10 lines and compares it against your demand forecast over 3 years (2026–2028).

Capacity formula per line:
Capacity (MT) = Speed (m/min) × Efficiency% × Shifts × 8hrs × 60min × Days × (1 − Downtime%) × kg/m ÷ 1000

Three units available: MT/month (primary), PCS/month, and Metres/month. Toggle using the top bar. All charts, KPIs, and tables switch together.

Schedule types: "Always On" lines run every shift. "Rotated" lines only activate when shifts ≥ 2. You can change any line's schedule in the Line Analysis tab.
Tab by tab guide
Capacity Forecast
The main view. Combo chart shows demand bars vs capacity line. Red bars = months where demand exceeds capacity. Use the year filter (2026/2027/2028) to zoom in. The cumulative gap chart shows whether you are building up a surplus or deficit over time. Traffic lights give a quick 36-month health overview.
Line Analysis
Adjust efficiency for each of the 10 lines using the sliders. The gap label shows how your planned efficiency compares to the historical baseline from Jan–May data. Toggle any line between Always On and Rotated — the capacity calculation updates instantly.
Conversion Factors
Edit kg/m and m/pcs per line if your product mix changes. Speed (m/min) is a machine spec and stays fixed. The summary table shows each line's capacity in all three units side by side.
Demand Editor
Type custom demand directly into any month cell. Orange values = custom override. Grey = calculated from the growth formula. Click Reset to go back to formula. Changing the base demand slider or growth rate in the sidebar updates all non-custom months.
ROI Calculator
Enable the expansion line in the sidebar first. Then enter your capex, monthly opex, revenue per MT, and penalty cost for unmet demand. The model calculates NPV, payback period, and cumulative cash flow. All values are in USD — adjust revenue per MT to match your pricing.
Intervention Planner
Lists all 36 months. Red rows = over capacity, amber = approaching limit. Type an action note against any month and click the flag button to mark it for follow-up. Notes are saved with your scenario.
Data Import
Two separate upload zones. Production actuals (uploaded by operations after month-end) updates line baselines and working days. Demand forecast (uploaded by sales monthly) fills in forward demand with confidence levels. Always review the preview before clicking Apply.

Tips & notes
· Scenarios are saved to your browser — they persist between sessions but are device-specific. Export important scenarios as PDF before sharing.
· The ROI calculator only activates when the expansion line toggle is ON in the sidebar.
· Baseline % comes from Jan–May 2025 actual production data uploaded from your ERP export.
· If demand exceeds 100% utilization, the line is over capacity — this signals you need either more shifts, higher efficiency, or a new line.
· The dark/light mode toggle is in the top bar. The app opens in dark mode by default.
